# Colors

Colors are important!

```.jsx
<div>
  <ColorPalette
    color={colors.OONI_BLUE}
    name="Primary: OONI Blue"
  />
  <ColorPalette
    color={colors.LIGHT_BLUE}
    name="Secondary: Light Blue"
  />
  <ColorPalette
    color={colors.BLACK}
    name="Black"
  />
  <ColorPalette
    color={colors.WHITE}
    name="White"
  />
</div>
```
